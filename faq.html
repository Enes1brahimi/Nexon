Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What are your shipping rates?

We offer free shipping on orders over $50. For orders under $50, the standard shipping fee is $5.99. Express shipping is available for $12.99, with delivery in 2-3 business days.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. International orders may take 10-14 business days depending on the destination.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package in real-time on our website or the carrier's website.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers for orders over $100.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! We use SSL encryption and PCI DSS compliance to protect your payment information. Your data is never stored on our servers and is processed securely through encrypted channels.

Do you offer installment payments?

Yes! For orders over $200, we offer 3, 6, or 12-month installment plans through select payment partners. Interest-free options are available for qualified purchases.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in resalable condition. Simply contact our support team to initiate a return.

Do you cover return shipping?

For defective or damaged items, we provide a pre-paid return label. For returns due to change of mind, return shipping is the customer's responsibility. However, returns over $100 include free return shipping.

How long does a refund take?

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we process the refund within 3-5 business days. The refund will appear in your account within 5-7 business days depending on your financial institution.

What warranty do products come with?

All products come with a standard 1-year manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Extended warranty options (2-3 years) are available for purchase at checkout.

Does the warranty cover accidental damage?

The standard warranty covers manufacturing defects only. However, we offer optional accidental damage protection plans that cover drops, spills, and other accidents. Customers can add this at checkout.

How do I claim warranty service?

To claim warranty service, visit our support page and upload photos of the defective item. Our team will review your claim and either authorize a repair, replacement, or refund within 24-48 hours.

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation, then enter your email and create a password. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration.

Is creating an account required to purchase?

No, you can check out as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, save items to your wishlist, and receive personalized recommendations.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a reset link. Click the link and create a new password within 24 hours.
